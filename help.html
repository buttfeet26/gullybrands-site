Shipping & Delivery

Every Gully Brands piece is printed on demand — made fresh for you when you order.

  • Production: your order goes into print within 24–48 hours of confirmation.
  • Delivery: 3–5 working days after dispatch, anywhere in India (27,000+ pin codes served).
  • Charges: FREE shipping on all prepaid orders. Cash on Delivery orders carry a ₹99 COD fee.
  • Tracking: you'll get an email + tracking link (AWB) as soon as your order ships.

If your order hasn't arrived within 10 days of the shipping email, write to us with your order ID and we'll chase it down.

Returns & Exchange

Because every product is printed on demand just for you, we don't accept returns for change of mind or wrong size selection — please check the size guide below before ordering.

We WILL replace or refund, no drama, if:

  • the product arrives damaged or defective,
  • the print is wrong or badly misprinted,
  • you received the wrong item or size (our mistake).

To claim: email us within 7 days of delivery with your order ID and clear photos of the issue. Approved claims get a free replacement, or a full refund to your original payment method (COD refunds via UPI/bank transfer) within 7–10 working days.
